About Hexryx

Hexryx Cybersecurity focuses on helping organizations improve cybersecurity awareness, identify risks, and strengthen security practices against modern cyber threats. The mission of Hexryx is to provide practical and effective cybersecurity guidance for businesses and individuals.

Services & Founder

Security Awareness Training

Hexryx provides cybersecurity awareness training focused on phishing attacks, password security, social engineering, email threats, safe browsing habits, and modern attack techniques. The goal is to reduce human error and improve employee awareness against real-world cyber threats.

Risk Assessments

Hexryx performs cybersecurity risk assessments designed to identify vulnerabilities, security weaknesses, and potential attack surfaces before they can be exploited. Assessments help organizations better understand their security posture and improve protection of systems, users, and sensitive information.

About the Founder

Hexryx was founded by Ronald White Jr., an honorably discharged U.S. Army veteran and cybersecurity graduate student at North Carolina A&T State University. His background includes experience as a Tactical Data Systems Specialist along with cybersecurity projects involving SIEM technologies, security awareness, security operations, and cybersecurity lab development.
