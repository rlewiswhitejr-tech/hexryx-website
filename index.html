Who We Are

Hexryx Cybersecurity focuses on helping businesses and individuals improve security awareness, identify vulnerabilities, and strengthen protection against modern cyber threats.

Services & Founder

Security Awareness Training

Hexryx provides cybersecurity awareness training focused on phishing attacks, password security, social engineering, email threats, and modern attack techniques. Training helps organizations reduce human error and improve employee readiness against real-world cyber threats.

Risk Assessments

Cybersecurity risk assessments help identify vulnerabilities, security weaknesses, and attack surfaces before they can be exploited. Hexryx helps organizations better understand their security posture and improve protection of users, systems, and sensitive data.

About the Founder
